This is the Chooser, (c) 2017 pocketsprite.com.

The software this console is delivered with builds on several open-source tools. This page attempts to credit them all. (Be aware that the links may not work while you're still connected to the console)

Various programs use uGUI for some display tasks. uGUI is a free/libre gui library by Achim Doebler.

The Game Boy emulator is based on Gnuboy created by Laguna which is a GPLv2 Gameboy/GBC emulator.

The Game Gear emulator is based on SMSPlus by Charles MacDonald and licensed under the GPLv2. The Z80 core used in the version running here is based on the one from the EightyOne emulator for the Sinclair ZX80 family of computers and is licensed under the GPLv2 or later.

The port of 2048 to the Gameboy is made by Sanqui and licensed under the zlib license

Geometrix is a game by Antonio Nino Diaz and licensed under the GPL.

Data Storm is made by Haroldo de Oliveira Pinheiro and licensed under the Apache 2.0 License.

To get the sources to this and more software, reconnect to the Internet and go to https://github.com/PocketSprite.
